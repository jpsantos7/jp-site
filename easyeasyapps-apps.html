Easy Easy Apps App Creation

Once Easy Easy Apps got some traction with the public there was a need to create some professional services.

The Easy Easy Apps pro services consisted on a team of developers (yours truly included) to make custom made apps.

It's in fact for the more complex apps or just for the people who want to have a custom built app with everything they're looking for (and looking good) for just a bit more money.

This is a project in which I was the lead designer and was responsible for a number of things, namely:

  • Prototyping (Low-res and hi-def mockups)
  • UX Design (for all the pro services apps)
  • UI Design (for all the pro services apps using HTML5, CSS3 using SASS and Bootstrap)
  • Writing and translating copy in english, spanish and brazilian portuguese
  • Creation of App templates to fit all general purposes
  • Writing all the default app's markup and style (HTML5 and CSS3 using SASS and Bootstrap)
  • Logo creation for the clients that needed it
  • Creating all the necessary graphic assets needed to publish an app (splashscreens & icons)
  • App publishing (on Apple App Store and Google Play)
  • Promote Easy Easy Apps in Tech Fairs
  • Create all the design material for fairs (t-shirts, roll-ups, posters, videos, flyers, business cards, etc.)
  • Client support

It has been quite fun working on the many varied apps for the very different clients.

Every app is like a small project and the team to juggle about 10 at a time a make sure everyone was satisfied and getting things done in time. Stressful but at the same time diversified.
